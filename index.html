Privacy Policy for AlgoViz

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

AlgoViz ("the App") is an offline educational application designed to help users visualize and understand algorithms. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data.

2. No Data Collection

AlgoViz does not collect, store, process, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

  • No account required
  • No internet connection required
  • No analytics used
  • No personal information collected
  • No device identifiers collected

3. No Third-Party Services

AlgoViz does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data, including but not limited to:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase
  • Analytics tools
  • Ad networks

4. Offline Functionality

The App works entirely offline, and all interactions remain on the user’s device.

5. Children's Privacy

AlgoViz is an educational application and may be used by children. Since the App collects no data, it complies with COPPA and Google Play's Families Policy Requirements.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when necessary. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
